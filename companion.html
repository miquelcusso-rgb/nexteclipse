How it looks on your screen

🍎 iPhone & iPad Safari only
1 Open next-eclipse.com in SafariChrome and Firefox don't support this on iOS.
2 Tap the Share ↑ button at the bottom of Safari
3 Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4 Give it a name and tap "Add" — the icon appears immediately
🤖 Android Using Chrome
1 Open next-eclipse.com in Chrome
2 Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots) in the top right
3 Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
4 Confirm — the eclipse icon appears on your home screen
