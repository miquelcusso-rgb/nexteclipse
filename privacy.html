Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What we collect

Next Eclipse does not collect personal data. The site does not require registration, does not ask for names or email addresses, and does not store user information on any server.

Language preference is saved in your browser's localStorage so the site remembers your choice on future visits. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted.

Cookies and advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display contextual advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show ads based on your visits to this and other websites. To opt out of personalised advertising, visit adssettings.google.com.

For more information about how Google uses data, see Google's privacy policy.

External services

Google Fonts is used to load Cinzel and Cormorant Garamond typefaces. Your browser may send a request to Google's servers when loading the page.

Eclipse data is sourced from NASA Goddard Space Flight Center (eclipse.gsfc.nasa.gov) and is not transmitted from your device.

Third-party links

This site links to nextfullmoon.co and lunallena.co. These sites have their own privacy policies.

Your rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to access, rectify or delete. If you have questions, contact us at hello@next-eclipse.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority. In Spain: Agencia Española de Protección de Datos (aepd.es).

Changes to this policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this page. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of any revised policy.
